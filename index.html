About Us

Company Overview

HEYDEN PETROLEUM LIMITED was incorporated in 30th October 2001 as an independent Oil Trading Company in Nigeria and in the United Kingdom. Our trading activities range from refined white products to semi-refined black products. We explore our formidable network structure to access and expand business relationships wherever profitable and feasible while promoting and sustaining professionalism. HPL is actively involved in the trading and distribution of Automotive Gas Oil, Diesel, Fuel Oils, Bitumen, Crude and some intermediate components within and outside Nigeria. HPL continues to negotiate strategic cooperation and agreement with several refineries in UAE, Europe and Russia which enables HPL to procure at discounted prices that allows us to offer unbeatable selling prices in the market place.
